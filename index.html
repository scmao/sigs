Putting your signature into Gmail:

  1. Copy your created signature and navigate to Gmail, open settings and go to the "General" tab.
  2. In the "Signature:" section select everything in the box and replace it with your Leanplum signature by pressing CMND + V
  3. Make sure the check box is checked under the input:
    "Insert this signature before quoted text in replies and remove the "--" line that precedes it."
  4. Save your changes
